## Design Context

### Users
- IPL fans, auction hosts, and cricket analysts exploring player records quickly during squad planning and debate.
- Typical context is fast scanning on laptop or phone while comparing roles, form proxies, and team fit.

### Brand Personality
- Competitive, confident, and data-forward.
- Should feel like a premium sports intelligence surface, not an admin dashboard.
- Emotional tone: energetic clarity with trustworthy numbers.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Editorial sports-data look with warm neutral surfaces and vivid field-inspired accents.
- Light-first visual system with layered gradients and textured backgrounds for atmosphere.
- Anti-reference: generic dark neon dashboards, repetitive card walls, and default UI kits.

### Design Principles
- Prioritize scan speed: key stats should be legible in under three seconds.
- Use role-aware hierarchy: batters, bowlers, and all-rounders should feel intentionally distinct.
- Keep interactions simple and direct: fewer clicks, richer at-a-glance detail.
- Build responsive parity: mobile retains core utility, not a reduced experience.
- Preserve data trust: avoid decorative flourishes that obscure numeric meaning.
